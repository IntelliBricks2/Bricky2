About Me!

Hi my name is Bricky! Welcome to my website! I am the mascot for IntelliBricks! I love Legos, video games, drawing, and coding.
My favorite thing to do is teach STEM to children of all ages. I really like when I can cheer young learners on their STEM path.
I wear a blue shirt and all of my IntelliBricks members wear bright yellow shirts.

Why I like teaching

I don't like teaching, I LOVE IT ; there is an amazing feeling that you get when you can pass on your knowledge to the next generation.
In doing so we can make the next generation more intelligent than when we were their age.

My favorite classes to teach

My favorite classes to teach are the LEGO classes because we can build and code to make our creations come to life!
The newest classes that I have created with the IntelliBricks team are the Digital Drawing and Web Development course.
I feel like these classes are the most helpful in balancing fun and important skills for later on in life.
